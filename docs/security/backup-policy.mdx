# Backup Policy
Regular backups are essential for data recovery and business continuity.

* **Backup Frequency:** Perform daily incremental backups and weekly full backups of critical data.
* **Retention Period:** Retain backups for a minimum of 30 days. Monthly backups should be kept for one year.
* **Storage Locations:** Store backups in multiple locations (on-site and off-site/cloud) to prevent data loss due to disasters.
* **Testing:** Conduct quarterly restore tests to ensure backup integrity and reliability.
* **Encryption:** All backups must be encrypted both in transit and at rest to protect sensitive information.
